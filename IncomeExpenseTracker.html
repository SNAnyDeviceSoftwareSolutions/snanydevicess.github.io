See your transactions at glance

From dashboard, you can see all your incomes and expeneses for current month. Tap on any date to expand or collapse group of records.

Using the button from Top-left corner, you can apply more filters. Similary you can expand or collapse all groups using second button from top-left corner.

In order to add a transaction, tap on plus button (+) which is placed at top-right corner.
